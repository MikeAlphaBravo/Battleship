Battleship, A New Frontier!!

Rules:

To play this online version of battleship 2 players must participate. Select your coordinates on the grid provided. The coordinates will represent the different ships and must be horizontal or vertical (no diagonal placement). You will select spaces on the table grid to represent 5 ships: the carrier (5 cells), the battleship (4 cells), the cruiser (3 cells), the submarine (3 cells), and the destroyer (2 cells). If you guess a coordinate with a ship on it you will recieve an alert for HIT(red)!, if not you will see a MISS(white). When you guess all locations correctly and have sunk all opponent's ships you will be the Battleship MASTER! In order to keep game play moving forward DO NOT refresh the browser.
